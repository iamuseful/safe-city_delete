Request Account Deletion – Safe City App

If you are a registered user of the Safe City app and wish to delete your account and all associated personal data, you may do so by contacting us through the instructions below.

What Will Be Deleted

Upon account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed from our servers:

  • Full name
  • Phone number
  • Location history
  • Distress audio recordings
  • Images captured during emergencies
  • SOS contact information

How to Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your Safe City account, please send an email to:

Email: support@safecity.ng

Account Deletion Request – Safe City App

Include your registered phone number in the message to verify your request.

Retention Policy

Your data will be deleted within 7 business days of receiving your verified request, unless required by law for investigation or legal purposes.

If you need further assistance, contact us via our support page or within the app's Help section.
